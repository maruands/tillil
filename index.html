Who we are

Arus Energy Limited is a Kenyan based company focused on renewable energy. Arus Energy has been granted a geothermal resource license by the Ministry of Energy in August 2018. The license which is valid for a 30 year period grants the company rights to prospect and develop geothermal energy in the 800 sq km Arus Bogoria prospect.

Arus Energy has title to 1,200 acres of land where Arus Steam Jets are located and an additional 80 sq km allocated by the Government of Kenya in the area. The area is in the Baringo County, Rift Valley and is located approximately 40Km Nakuru town. Our site is easily accessible via road and connection to the grid will be made possible through the Silali-Rongai 400Kv Line scheduled for completion in 2018.
